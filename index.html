Speculative Biology Magazine

Astrovitae is a digital publication focused on speculative biology, art, and science fiction. The magazine is planned to be released biannually and include a variety of different artists and creators. The goal of the magazine is to connect the community with speculative biology artists around the globe and highlight their amazing projects. If you are interested in submitting your own work to feature in an issue, see the submissions page for detailed information.

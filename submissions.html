Submission Information:

Thank you for taking interest in submitting work for publication! Here are some steps one can take to prepare an entry for review. For a detailed description on how to participate in the magazine please download our official guidelines below:

Offical Guidelines

Preparation:

1.) Decide on the Type of Content:

Take a look at a past issue, featured artist, or refrence the official guidelines to get an idea of what kind of content is appropriate for the magazine. Observe your art or your project critically and begin brainstorming what you would like to include in an issue!

2.) Find Images:

Gather images you wish to show in your submission. Remember that your images should be high-quality (at least 300 dpi resolution) or close to this. Photographs should be properly exposed and edited before being submitted.

3.) Start Writing:

Create a write-up that is roughly the word-length of the type of entry you plan to submit (see the official guidelines for more information). Include a title for your entry, your full name (or whatever you wish to be referred as), and your Instagram or social media username. Make sure that your content is checked for any spelling or grammar mistakes before submitting it!

4.) Submit your Entry:

Your text document should be sent via email as a .pdf file, .docx file, or as a google doc link. Images should be included with your email or can be sent in a separate email with a matching subject line. You must be 16+ years of age to enter a submission. Entries may be rejected if they do not meet a certain quality or do not meet the suggested word count (see the official guidelines for more information).

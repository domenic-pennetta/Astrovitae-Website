Submission Information:

Thank you for your interest in submitting work to the magazine! For a detailed description of submission requirements, please download our official guidelines below:

Offical Guidelines

Submission Info:

Submission Deadlines:

For April Release - Submissions due March 15th 11:59 PM EST
For September Release - Submissions due August 15th at 11:59 PM EST

Submission Requirements:

Your full first and last name. If you are uncomfortable with using your real name, then please provide a pseudonym or username.

Title of your article or project.

Links to your website, portfolio, or social media accounts.

A text document as a .pdf file, .docx file, or as an accessible google doc link.

High quality images as a .png file or a .jpeg file (images must be at least 300dpi resolution).

Please be aware of the submission requirements above. Failure to follow the requirements may lead to the rejection of your submission.

Submit your Entry:

Send your files to astrovitaeofficial@gmail.com.

For any questions, feel free to send an email to the address provided above.

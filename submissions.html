Submission Information:

Thank you for taking interest in submitting work for publication! Here are some steps one can take to prepare an entry for review. For a detailed description on how to participate in the magazine please download our official guidelines below:

Offical Guidelines

Preparation:

1.) Decide on the Type of Content:

Take a look at a past issue, a featured artist, or reference the official guidelines to get an idea of what kind of content is appropriate for the magazine. Observe your own work critically and begin brainstorming what you would like to include in an issue!

2.) Gather Images:

Find images you wish to show in your submission. Remember that your images should be high-quality (at least 300 dpi resolution) or close to this. Photographs should be properly exposed and edited before being submitted.

3.) Start Writing:

Before making a submission make sure that you have created and sent a written abstract about your work. Abstracts will be reviewed and only a few will be selected. However, do not feel discouraged from rejection as you are free to submit again for each issue. If accepted, you will be able to begin writing your submission. (See the official guidelines above for more information)

4.) Submit your Entry:

Your text document should be sent via email as a .pdf file, .docx file, or as an accessible google documents link. You should be 18+ years of age to enter a submission, if you are younger than 18 then you will need a parent or guardianâ€™s permission.
